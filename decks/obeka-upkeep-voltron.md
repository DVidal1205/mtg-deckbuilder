## Strategy

Obeka deals combat damage to a player → we get that many **additional upkeep steps** this turn. The deck is two engines bolted together:

1. **Voltron Obeka** — stack evasion and a power buff on a 2/5 menace body, then swing. Even 2 damage equals 2 extra upkeeps; a pumped, double-striking hit triples or quadruples every trigger.
2. **Upkeep trigger value** — every permanent that ticks on our upkeep (draw, tokens, drains, mana, venture, recursion, copy effects) fires again for each extra step. Value snowballs into the game-ending turn and acts as a backup win even if Obeka gets locked out.

The D&D Initiative subtheme is the cleanest in-format synergy with this commander: _"At the beginning of your upkeep, venture into Undercity."_ Three dungeoneers plus `Sarevok's Tome` give us four ways to grab the Initiative.

**No self-harm on upkeep.** Every "at the beginning of your upkeep" effect is either neutral (draw, mana, venture, recursion), gain life, or harms opponents only. No cumulative upkeep.

## Key Synergies

- **`Strionic Resonator` + Obeka's damage trigger** — pay `{2}` to copy her combat-damage trigger. A 4-damage Obeka swing that would yield 4 extra upkeeps now yields **8**. Obviously it also doubles court triggers, Sheoldred reanimates, Mechanized Production copies, Oversold Cemetery returns, and every other upkeep payoff in the deck.
- **`Moraug, Fury of Akoum` + `Crucible of Worlds` + fetches** — landfall gives an extra combat phase, and each fetch cracks for two landfall triggers (fetch enters + the fetched land enters). With 4 fetches in the deck that's up to 8 extra combats over a game, each with an Obeka swing generating her own extra upkeeps. Crucible recycles the spent fetches out of the graveyard for further turns. Moraug also untaps creatures at each extra combat and pumps them +1/+0 per combat they've attacked in — a 4-combat Obeka is +3/+0 on swing 4, before any equipment.
- **Double-Obeka engine (`Irma, Part-Time Mutant`)** — Irma becomes a copy of Obeka at beginning of combat. Two attackers, each with Obeka's ability, each generating independent extra upkeeps.
- **`Consecrated Sphinx` + `Rhystic Study` + `Venser's Journal`** — the hand-flood + lifegain shell. Sphinx and Study refill the hand; Journal converts hand size into 30+ life per combat.
- **`Braids, Conjurer Adept` + Obeka extras** — "each player's upkeep, may put an artifact, creature, or land into play." Opponents cheat once per their turn; we cheat per upkeep step, which means 3–5 free permanents on any turn Obeka attacks.
- **`Extravagant Replication` + `Mechanized Production`** — Replication copies any nonland opponent permanent per upkeep; Production copies our own chosen artifact per upkeep. With Obeka's extras, Production hits the 8-copy alt-win in a single attack turn.
- **Recursion stack (`Sheoldred`, `Palace Siege`, `Oversold Cemetery`, `The Soul Stone` harnessed)** — upkeep creature reanimation / return; `Crucible` + fetches for lands; `Seize the Day` flashback for sorceries.
- **Court cycle (`Cunning`, `Ire`, `Embereth`, `Locthwain`, `Ambition`)** — five monarch-on-upkeep triggers. `Locthwain` gives free casts from exile if monarch.
- **Obeka + double strike (`Temur Battle Rage`, `Embercleave`)** — first-strike damage triggers Obeka, then regular damage triggers her again.
- **Obeka + `Hatred`** — classic Voltron kill button. 30 life of headroom from `Venser's Journal` + `Silver Shroud Costume` evasion = lethal commander damage swing.
- **Obeka + `Sphinx of the Second Sun`** — Sphinx adds an extra beginning phase on our postcombat main: untap all permanents + upkeep triggers fire again + draw a card. It's a per-turn engine, not a guaranteed extra combat, but the "untap everything and trigger every engine a second time" still plays like a soft Time Walk.
- **`Creeping Bloodsucker` scaling lifegain** — 1 life gained per upkeep baseline, but with Obeka's extra upkeeps that becomes 4–6 life per attack turn. Small per-trigger, huge cumulative, and it drains every opponent each tick.
- **`Silent Arbiter` + Voltron** — opponents can only block with one creature, so menace-plus-anything-else gets Obeka through clean.
- **`Brotherhood Regalia` on Obeka** — equip-legendary costs just `{1}`, and the equipped creature "has ward {2} and can't be blocked." That's a one-mana unblockable + ward Voltron suit stapled onto a 2-MV artifact. Best unblockable enabler in the deck by a mile.
- **Ring cycle + Obeka's UBR identity** — `Ring of Evos Isle` (blue) and `Ring of Valkas` (red) each drop a `+1/+1` counter on equipped Obeka every upkeep (both color triggers hit — she's blue AND red AND black). Stacked on Obeka, that's **2 permanent counters per upkeep**, multiplied by every extra upkeep she generates. After one 4-damage attack, Obeka gains 10 counters on the turn. Plus repeatable hexproof and granted haste as free utility.

## Notes

- **Game changers (B3-legal, using 3/3):** `Fierce Guardianship`, `Rhystic Study`, `Profane Tutor`. Max allotment; `Profane Tutor` suspends 2 and accelerates off Obeka's extra upkeeps.
- **Tutors:** just `Profane Tutor`. Otherwise we trust redundancy.
- **Ramp package:** `Sol Ring`, `Arcane Signet`, two signets, two talismans, `The Soul Stone` (indestructible), `Replicating Ring` (scales with upkeeps). Consistent turn-4 Obeka.
- **Upkeep recursion:** `Sheoldred` (battlefield), `Palace Siege` (to hand or drain), `Oversold Cemetery` (to hand, 4+ creature cards in yard), `The Soul Stone` harnessed (battlefield). Creature-focused; lands/sorceries via `Crucible` / `Seize the Day` flashback.
- **Offensive instants & sorceries:** `Temur Battle Rage`, `Seize the Day` (flashback extra combat), `Hatred` (X-pump kill); `Embercleave` and `Lizard Blades` for double strike. Moraug's landfall provides the repeatable extra-combat engine.
- **Protection layer:** `Swiftfoot Boots`, `Silver Shroud Costume`, `Deflecting Swat`, `Fierce Guardianship`, `Swan Song`.
- **Evasion:** `Aqueous Form`, `Silver Shroud Costume`, `Rogue's Passage`, `Shizo, Death's Storehouse`, plus double-strike tricks and `Silent Arbiter`'s one-blocker limit.
- **Win conditions:** primary = 21 commander damage closed by `Hatred` or equipment stack. Secondary = `Mechanized Production` alt-win (8 artifact copies), completed Undercity, the value-engine flood.
- **Mana base:** full fetch/dual/shock shell tilted to UB+BR+UR (no green fetches), all three Grixis battlebonds, two triomes, `Arena of Glory` for surprise-haste Obeka, `Rogue's Passage`/`Shizo` for unblockable finishers. 6 basics support the 4 fetches.

Cut `Blasphemous Act` (redundant wipe) and `Ill-Gotten Inheritance` (weakest drain, redundant with `Creeping Bloodsucker` and courts). Added `Command Beacon` (commander-tax reset — Obeka will die repeatedly as the voltron target) and a third `Swamp` to support BB double-pip density.
- **2026-04-20 (v9):** Trimmed 111 → 100 after the user-added pile. Cuts for redundancy and curve smoothing:
    - **Illegal:** `Grave Researcher` (Secrets of Strixhaven is not Commander-legal).
    - **Reanimator redundancy:** `Virtue of Persistence` (7 MV — duplicates `Sheoldred` for one more mana).
    - **Top-end redundancy:** `Skyline Despot` (7 MV monarch — already have 5 courts for monarch).
    - **Group-hug foot-gun:** `Kumena's Awakening` (feeds opponents' hands, contradicts `Rhystic Study`).
    - **Weaker impulse:** `Sin Prodder` (opp-choice makes it the worst of our three reveal engines — `Twilight Prophet` and `Plargg and Nassari` both strictly outdo it).
    - **Conditional unblockable:** `Sword Coast Sailor` (only works when attacking the lowest-life opponent — `Brotherhood Regalia`, `Aqueous Form`, and `Thassa`'s activated ability are all unconditional).
    - **Obsoleted equipment:** `Trailblazer's Boots` and `Winged Boots` (`Brotherhood Regalia` is unblockable with ward 2 for `{1}` equip, strictly better).
    - **Dead early-game:** `Dragonmaster Outcast` (needs 6+ lands; does nothing turns 1–5 — exactly the window we wanted to fill).
    - **Weakest ring:** `Ring of Xathrid` (regenerate is niche vs modern exile; kept Evos for hexproof and Valkas for haste, both of which still drop counters on Obeka each upkeep).
    - **Redundant unblockable:** `Artful Dodge` (sorcery unblockable + flashback is outclassed by `Aqueous Form`'s permanent version at identical `{1}{U}`).
    - **Net effect:** avg MV drops from 3.50 → ~3.14, ≤4-MV count goes from 47 → 46 (stays dense for turns 1–4), top-heavy 7+ slot goes from 4 → 2.
