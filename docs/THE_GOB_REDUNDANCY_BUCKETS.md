# The Gob - Redundancy Buckets

Deck snapshot analyzed: current `decks/the-gob.md` (**103 cards total**, after latest cuts).

## How to read this

- **Efficiency** = how well the card performs its primary bucket job relative to similar options in this same deck.
- Scale: **S / A / B / C / D**
  - **S** = best-in-bucket for rate/impact
  - **A** = strong, usually keep
  - **B** = good but replaceable
  - **C** = functional redundancy, candidate cut
  - **D** = low floor or overcosted for job

## Quick refresh for current list (103 -> 100)

You already removed a lot of low-floor redundancy (`Goro-Goro`, `Torch Courier`, `Battle Cry Goblin`, `Foundry Street Denizen`, `Infernal Plunge`, `Lotus Petal`, `Bitter Reunion`, `Tibalt's Trickery`, and `Den of the Bugbear`).

That means the remaining easiest trims are now mostly in **redundant one-shot burn**, **variance interaction**, and **slow/meta-flex slots**.

### Best 3 candidate cuts right now

1. **`Massive Raid`** (`1RR` instant: deals damage equal to creatures you control)  
   - **Bucket:** one-shot burn finisher redundancy  
   - **Why now:** overlaps heavily with `Mob Justice` and `Goblin War Strike`; weakest rate in that trio for how often it closes games.

2. **`Wild Magic Surge`** (`RR` instant: destroy permanent, controller flips to random same type)  
   - **Bucket:** broad interaction  
   - **Why now:** you already have `Chaos Warp` as the premium catch-all; this is the higher-variance copy.

3. **`Hexing Squelcher`** (`1R` creature: your spells can't be countered; your creatures get ward-pay-2-life)  
   - **Bucket:** protection/meta slot  
   - **Why now:** strong in blue-heavy pods, but low impact in pods with fewer counterspells and doesn't advance tokens/draw directly.

### If you want alternates (same tier)

- **`Goblin War Strike`** (`R` sorcery-speed one-shot damage to player/planeswalker = Goblin count)  
  Keep either this **or** `Mob Justice`; running both plus `Massive Raid` is usually excess.
- **`Amonkhet Raceway`** (colorless land; haste mode gated by max-speed)  
  Fine utility, but lower consistency than a clean red source at this power level.
- **`Marvin, Murderous Mimic`** (`2` artifact creature with copied activated abilities)  
  High ceiling, inconsistent floor; easier cut if you want fewer setup pieces.

## 16) Quick "weakest-in-bucket" watchlist (not auto-cuts)

These are the most likely trim points when you need slots, based on redundancy + lower efficiency:

- `Torch Courier` (one-shot haste)
- `Lotus Petal` (one-shot ramp)
- `Infernal Plunge` (high-risk ritual)
- `Goblin War Strike` or `Massive Raid` (one-shot burn redundancy)
- `Tibalt's Trickery` (variance)
- `Hexing Squelcher` (meta-dependent)
- `Amonkhet Raceway` / `Muraganda Raceway` / `Hidden Volcano` (slower land quality vs optimized B3 starts)
- `Banner of Kinship` (expensive payoff in already-full payoff bucket)
- `Marvin, Murderous Mimic` (inconsistent floor)

This section is only to highlight likely pressure points; keep any of these if they are overperforming in your pod.
