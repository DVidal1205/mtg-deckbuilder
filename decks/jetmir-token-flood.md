## Strategy

1. **Develop early board presence** with one-mana dorks and cheap token engines so we hit Jetmir's first threshold (3 creatures) quickly.
2. **Scale into critical mass** with repeatable token makers and token doublers, then land Jetmir as the payoff to convert wide boards into immediate lethal pressure.
3. **Protect and close** by forcing through combat with trample/double strike and using protection instants to punish wipe-heavy tables.

This is a bracket-3 combat deck: optimized mana, efficient interaction, and explosive turns without infinite-combo focus.

## Key Synergies

- **Jetmir + token bursts** (`March of the Multitudes`, `Grand Crescendo`, `Secure the Wastes`) turns a stable board into sudden 9+ creature lethality.
- **Token doublers** (`Anointed Procession`, `Parallel Lives`, `Mondrak, Glory Dominus`) scale every generator into exponential board growth.
- **Attack engines** (`Adeline, Resplendent Cathar`, `Rabble Rousing`, `Krenko, Tin Street Kingpin`) naturally snowball combat steps.
- **Draw from going wide** (`Skullclamp`, `Idol of Oblivion`, `Rumor Gatherer`, `Welcoming Vampire`, `Shamanic Revelation`) keeps gas flowing.
- **Protection layer** (`Heroic Intervention`, `Flawless Maneuver`, `Grand Crescendo`) preserves tempo through sweepers.

## Notes

- **Game changers:** `Gaea's Cradle`, `Aura Shards`, `Field of the Dead`.
- **Tutor density:** none in maindeck (keeps B3 profile focused on board development over consistency loops).
- **Interaction package:** efficient spot removal plus flexible answers for problematic permanents.
- **Mana philosophy:** high untapped source density, full fetch/dual shell, and creature acceleration to cast Jetmir on curve.
