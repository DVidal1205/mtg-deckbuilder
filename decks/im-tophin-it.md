## Strategy

1. **Stick Toph** — Lands become artifacts. Every land drop creates a 3/3 Golem. Ramp early (Sol Ring, Mox Opal, Lotus Petal, Chromatic Lantern) and protect her (Swiftfoot Boots, Heroic Intervention, Deflecting Swat, Flawless Maneuver).
2. **Stack landfall + artifact synergies** — Dryad of the Ilysian Grove smooths land types. Field of the Dead adds Zombies. Scute Swarm, Felidar Retreat, and landfall payoffs layer triggers. Evolution Sage proliferates counters on every landfall.
3. **Close the game** — Moraug gives extra combats on landfall. All Will Be One + Evolution Sage creates a damage engine. Big Mother Mouser and The Ooze add counter and token density; Planar Engineering can spike multiple land ETBs in one turn. Swing with a massive token army buffed by counter synergies.

B3 — no infinites. **Game changers:** Gaea's Cradle, The One Ring, Field of the Dead. **Tutors:** Sylvan Scrying, Expedition Map, Goblin Engineer.

## Key Synergies

- **Toph + Roaming Throne** (choose Avatar) — double Golem triggers per land drop.
- **Toph + Ashaya** — all creatures become lands AND artifacts. Artifact tutors find creatures.
- **Field of the Dead + Toph** — 2 tokens per landfall (Zombie + Golem) once at 7+ unique land names.
- **Evolution Sage + All Will Be One** — landfall → proliferate → deal damage equal to counters placed.
- **Liquimetal Coating / Torque** — turn opponents' permanents into artifacts for removal via artifact hate.
- **Wake the Past** — returns ALL artifacts from GY, which includes every land with Toph out.
- **Gaea's Cradle + token army** — massive mana; 20 tokens = 20 green mana.
- **The Ozolith + Resourceful Defense** — preserve counters when creatures die; nothing is wasted.
